### Welcome to Tables from Beyond!

I've always wanted a place on the internet to call my own, and this is it. I'm not entirely sure what the point of this website is, but I use it mostly to share personal content like short stories and TTRPG session reports.

If you're looking for a place to leave feedback or engage in discussions, you're outta luck. I'm too cheap to host a database to store comments, but more importantly, I don't really care for the feedback. This website is my personal space, and I ain't trying to hear anyone else's two cents.

But hey, if you're just here to consume some content, then you're in luck. I've got some killer short stories and TTRPG session reports for you to sink your teeth into. So go ahead and explore, and if you happen to find something you like, feel free to share it with others.

So yeah, thanks for stopping by. This website is a reflection of my passions and creations, and I'm excited to share them with you. Just don't get too excited and try to engage with me, because I'm like a one-way street - you can read my stuff, but you can't talk back. But hey, let's keep it chill and irreverent anyway, and maybe I'll hear your laughter from my undisclosed location.
